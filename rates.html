Rates & Policies

This page explains dispatch terms, cancellation policy, and pricing structure for JM Holler Hauling Services LLC serving Indiana • Ohio • Illinois.

Weekend‑Only Operations

JM Holler Hauling Services LLC operates as a scheduled, weekend‑only hauling and transport service. Limited availability means confirmed dispatches block scheduling for other customers.

Cancellation / Release Policy (Critical for Hot Shot Loads)

  • Cancellation after dispatch but before loading: $75 dispatch/cancellation fee applies.
  • If the vehicle/load is positioned for loading or partially secured: the full base load fee applies.
  • Once the load is fully secured: the minimum charge applies regardless of cancellation.

Long Haul / Interstate Hot Shot Pricing

Tuned for 75–600+ mile runs, fewer but higher value loads, and zero-dispute quoting.

Final Rate Structure

  • Base Load / Dispatch Fee: $150 (includes loading, securement, dispatch; first 50 loaded miles included)
  • Mileage: $2.25 per loaded mile (pickup → drop‑off only; no deadhead/return mileage)
  • Minimum Invoice: $200

Time & Priority Adders

  • Wait time: first 30 minutes free → $50/hr
  • After hours / weekends / holidays: +$75 flat
  • Expedited / time‑critical: +20% (quoted before dispatch)

Customer Acknowledgment

By requesting dispatch, the customer acknowledges and accepts the pricing and cancellation terms above and authorizes transport under these conditions. Digital or text acknowledgment may be accepted (example: replying “YES” to dispatch terms).

Dispute‑Proof Text Template

Pricing is $150 base incl. first 50 miles, then $2.25 per loaded mile, $200 minimum. Cancellation after dispatch $75. Reply YES to dispatch.

Tip: Send this before you roll. A “YES” reply wins disputes.
